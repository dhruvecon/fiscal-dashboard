Model — Commodity S&D: Qd = Ad·P-εd, Qs = As·Pεs + Bm. Demand ε: crude 0.10 (Kilian & Murphy 2014), diesel 0.10 (Dahl & Sterner 1991), petrol 0.15 (Labandeira et al 2017), LPG 0.08, LNG 0.15 (IEA), urea 0.05. Supply shocked: (1−λ)/wεs, λ = imp_dep × gulf_share × δ.
Corrections: LPG from PPAC regression ($/MT = 50.1+7.69×crude). Fertiliser: gas cost = (29 MMBtu × gas + $40) × exr, 70/30 dom/import, sub = cost − ₹5,378 MRP. Gulf share of urea imports = 70%. Gas rationing = 70% (Gas Control Order). Kharif front-loading applied.
Buffers (MMT): Crude 42.4 (74 days, PRS/MoPNG), Urea 6.2 (62 LMT), LPG 1.4 (~15d), LNG 0.5 — additive constants in supply equation.
Election: MCC from Mar 15. Assam/Kerala Apr 9, TN Apr 23, WB Apr 23+29, counting May 4 (ECI). No retail hike months 1–2.
